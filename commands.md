# Verse-Music Commands

 - `setup`: Setup the music player for the current channel.
 
 <hr>

 - `play`: Play a song or adds a song to the queue. Can take a URL or a search term as input.
 - `search`: Search for a song on YouTube and displays the search results.
 - `pause`: Pause/unpause the currently playing song.
 - `stop`: Stop playing music and clears the queue.
 - `skip`: Skip the currently playing song.
 - `skip-to`: Skip to a specific song in the queue.
 - `restart`: Restart the currently playing song.
 - `seek`: Seek to a specific time in the currently playing song.
 - `loop`: Toggle loop mode.
 - `volume`: Set the volume of the music player.
 - `autoplay`: Toggle autoplay on/off.
 
 <hr>

 - `queue`: Display the current queue.
 - `shuffle`: Shuffle the queue.
 - `clear`: Clear the queue.
 - `move`: Moves a song to a specific position in the queue.
 - `remove`: Remove a specific song by its position in the queue.
 - `remove-duplicate`: Remove all duplicate songs in the queue.

 <hr>

 - `save`: Save the currently playing song to the saved songs list.
 - `unsave`: Remove the current song from the saved songs list.
 - `saved`: Display a list of saved songs.
 - `save-queue`: Save the current queue as a playlist.
 - `clear-save`: Clear the entire saved songs list.
